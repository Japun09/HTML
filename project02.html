WHICH ITEMS FIT FOR SHIPPING IN A TRUCK WITH TEMPERATURE CONTROL?

Perishable goods will eventually deteriorate if they are exposed to extremes in humidity or temperature. The following perishable goods, among others, ought to be shipped in a temperature-controlled environment.

-Food and plants Most plants and vegetables will quickly ripen or wilt if they are shipped without the proper refrigeration. Ripe fruit may subsequently raise the temperature in ordinary trailers even more, causing products to decay faster than they would in chilled or frozen trailers.

-Derivatives from Animals Whether they are frozen or chilled, animal products must be shipped in a temperature-controlled environment. Establish the proper temperature for items like meat, eggs, and dairy goods by working with your 3PL supplier. -Medicinal Products Vaccines, medicines, and other pharmaceutical goods need to be frozen or refrigerated at specific temperatures in order for patients and healthcare providers to continue using them. The contents of the object may become spoilt and unusable due to temperature changes, which could be dangerous.

- Beautifying products Cosmetics can degrade, just like drugs, if they are heated or cooled above permissible ranges.
